Paolo Maldini

The Legendary Italian Defender

Paolo Maldini is one of the greatest defenders in football history. Born on June 26, 1968 in Milan, Italy, he spent his entire professional career at AC Milan and the Italian national team. His leadership, skill, and sportsmanship made him a true football icon.
